Things To Do Around Granbury.

You will find lots of sights, shopping and food around the Granbury square.

Granbury Square

With coffee shops, wine bars, and ice cream parlors, there is no end to the types and tastes of food you'll find. If you need to walk off some of that food, there is a fantastic walking trail through Shanley Park just off the square. Enjoy this historic town!

Dinosaur Park

Just a 15 mile drive down the road, leads you to Dinosaur Park. Where you can hike through the park being greeted by some of the smallest and the tallest dinosaurs of their time.
