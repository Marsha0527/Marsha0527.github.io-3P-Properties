Things To Do Around Paluxy.

You will find lots of sights, shopping and food around Granbury.

Granbury Square

On this historic square, you will find loads of shopping and food. With coffee shops, wine bars, ice cream parlors, there is no end to the types and tastes of food you'll find. If you need to walk off some of that food, there is a fantastic walking trail through Shanley Park just off the square. Enjoy!

Dinosaur Park

Just a 15 mile drive down the road, leads you to Dinosaur Park. Where you can hike through the park being greeted by some of the smallest and the tallest dinosaurs of their time.
